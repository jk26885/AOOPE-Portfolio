About This Portfolio

I'm a postgraduate student on the Advanced Object-Oriented Programming module, and this site is my e-portfolio for the course — a week-by-week record of the artefacts, design decisions, and reflections produced across the module.

How this site is organised

Each entry under Weeks in the sidebar corresponds to one unit of the module, it includes tasks, a reflection and outcomes. Where I was able to complete formative assignments, briefs are provided and links to the github repositories for the corresponding code.. Capstone draws these together into a single case study; Reflection and PDP cover the module's academic and professional-development requirements; References lists every source cited throughout.

Independent working

This e-Portfolio, and every artefact within it, was designed, implemented, tested, and documented independently as an individual project. All architectural decisions — including pattern selection, security implementation, and test strategy — were made and justified by me, and all code was written by me without collaborative authorship. Where published sources (see References) informed a decision, this is acknowledged explicitly in the relevant commentary rather than presented as independent insight.
