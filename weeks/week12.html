Week 12

Capstone Project and Review

In this final week of the module, I consolidated the knowledge and skills developed over the previous eleven weeks by completing my Capstone Project. The project provided an opportunity to apply advanced object-oriented programming concepts in practice, including object-oriented design principles, design patterns, secure coding techniques, software architecture, and testing strategies. Through the design and implementation of a complex software solution, I was able to demonstrate how these concepts work together to address real-world challenges. I also used this week to review and reflect on the key topics covered throughout the module, reinforcing my understanding of OOP fundamentals, architectural design, security considerations, and quality assurance practices. This process highlighted how the individual learning outcomes from each unit combined to support the development of a robust, maintainable, and scalable software system.
