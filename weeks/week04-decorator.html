Reflection

Week 4 Reflection

Unit 4 introduced me to structural design patterns and helped me understand how classes and objects can be organised to create more flexible and maintainable software systems. Through the reading materials, I learned how patterns such as Adapter, Bridge, Composite, Decorator, Facade, Proxy, and Flyweight solve common structural challenges by improving collaboration between components while reducing complexity.

This unit introduced structural design patterns and their role in creating modular and maintainable software systems. The readings reinforced the idea that design patterns provide reusable solutions to common software development challenges and can improve software maintainability and scalability (Di Francesco, 2024).

Outcome

Unit Outcome

By completing this week's exercises, I was able to develop an understanding of structural design patterns and how they can be used to create modular, flexible, and maintainable software systems by organising classes and objects into effective structures
